What is PollApp?

We all encounter situations where we want to ask a question and hear what others think. However, asking can be hard at times. We don't always have the target audience in reach, nor have the confidence to ask.

PollApp is an Android application and platform for asking whatever is on your mind, to an audience of your choice, in the form of a short and simple poll.

Polls are classified by topics so you can focus the polls you ask or answer to your personal interests, and you can easily view the interesting statistics on things you care about.

Why would I want to answer polls?

PollApp is a social game. You can't expect to have your questions answered, or watch real and representitive statistics, unless you take part in the game and answer some other polls. Also, taking active part in polls might be worthwhile. That we will discuss later on. We do understand there are many questions to be asked, and seeing too much of them can get annoying, so we enable you to customize the number of questions you see. Also, we take actions for decreasing repetitions of same questions.

We belive the more people are exposed to PollApp, the more interesting questions and accurate answers will be present. Therefore YOUR contibution to us is to share this to as many as your friends, download the app and become a part of the statistics (Well, in the good way...)
